Education

I didn't know what I wanted to study when I went to Colby-Sawyer College. I really enjoyed the Art History classes I took there so I transferred to URI where I got my Bachelor of Arts in Art History. I stayed at URI to get my Masters of Library and Information Science. Later, when I was working, I was able to take some courses at Rare Book School, which was a lot of fun.
